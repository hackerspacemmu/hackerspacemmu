HACKERSPACE MMU

ABOUT US

Hacking things together
Not (necessarily) hacking into things!

A hackerspace is a location where people with common interests, usually in computers, technology, science or digital art can meet, socialise and/or collaborate. A hackerspace can be viewed as open community labs incorporating elements of machine shops, workshops and/or studios where hackers can come together to share resources and knowledge to build and make things. We mostly building things, not so much breaking security.

MISSION


Hackerspace MMU aims to enable and encourage like-minded members of the MMU community to share ideas and explore them through collaborative or individual projects in the interest of learning and beyond.

JOIN US

We're not exactly made up of all pros :D we have many beginners as well. Although we mostly self-learn, but we do ask one another for some guidance esp when starting on something.

Sometimes being pointed in the right direction helps a lot. Quite a lot of people starting out with basics - like following a tutorial on the web, etc. But in Hackerspace, we always declare a project and work towards it. That's how we all learn.

You are welcome to join us to be part of our community. However, membership is exclusive to MMU students or staff only. You may sign up through application form provided below or contact us though email if you have any inquiries.

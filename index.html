A hackerspace is a location where people with common interests, usually in computers, technology, science or digital art can meet, socialise and/or collaborate. A hackerspace can be viewed as open community labs incorporating elements of machine shops, workshops and/or studios where hackers can come together to share resources and knowledge to build and make things.

We're not exactly made up of all pros :D we have many beginners as well. Although we mostly self-learn, but we do ask one another for some guidance esp when starting on something. Sometimes being pointed in the right direction helps a lot. Quite a lot of people starting out with basics - like following a tutorial on the web, etc.

But we always declare a project and work towards it. That's how we all learn. Feel free to join us to be part of our community. We truly welcome any MMU students or staff who enjoy learning things together.
